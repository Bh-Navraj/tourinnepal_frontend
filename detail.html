Annapurna Basecamp Trek

Annapurna Base camp trekking is situated northern central part of Nepal and also inside the Annapurna conservation area at an altitude of 4130 m on the lap of the world’s 10th Highest Mountain Annapurna I. Annapurna base camp is surrounded by the majestic Himalayas like Annapurna I, Annapurna II, Annapurna III, Machhapuchare, Hiunchuli, Gangapurna, Tent peak, and many other peaks.Annapurna Base camp trekking is situated northern central part of Nepal and also inside the Annapurna conservation area at an altitude of 4130 m on the lap of the world’s 10th Highest Mountain Annapurna I.

Trip Equipment

Equipment list

The Manaslu Circuit Trek is a blend of natural and cultural richness. The trail takes you through the spectacular Manaslu region and the people that call it home. It is challenging yet worthy of all the hard work. The total journey around the Manaslu takes 15 days and involves the crossing of the great Larkya La pass. This trek is one of the most popular off-beaten path trails among trekkers

Upper Body - Head / Ears / Eyes

  • Shade hat or baseball cap
  • Warm wool or synthetic hat that cover your ears
  • Glacier glasses (100% UV protection)
  • Headlamp or light torch light. Make sure to bring extra batteries.
